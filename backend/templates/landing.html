Free hosted trading card intake

GergVault

Upload front and back group photos, turn them into reviewable card records, and keep AI output draft-first until a human approves it.

Open the hosted app Star on GitHub

Batch Intake

Built for the practical collector workflow: one photo of card fronts, one photo of backs, then 10 draft rows by slot order.

Human Review First

AI extraction, crop matching, and value estimates stay draft/needs-review. Nothing is final until you approve it.

Open Source

The code is public, self-hostable, and ready for provider adapters, pricing research, and collection workflows.

Free to start, supported by donations

GergVault is free while the hosted version is early. If it saves you time cataloging cards, support helps cover hosting, AI experiments, pricing provider work, and better demo data.

Donation links are coming. For now, starring the repo and sharing it with collectors helps a lot.
