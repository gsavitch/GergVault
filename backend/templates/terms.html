GergVault Terms

GergVault is an early hosted beta of an open-source card intake tool. Use it for cataloging and review workflows, but do not treat AI extraction or pricing estimates as final authority.

Review Required

All AI metadata, crop matching, and estimated values are draft outputs. You are responsible for reviewing card records before relying on them.

Uploaded Content

Only upload card images and collection data you have the right to store. Do not upload sensitive personal documents or unrelated private information.

Availability

The hosted beta is free to start and may change while the product matures. Future paid plans may add larger limits, managed provider credits, exports, collaboration, and support.

Open Source

The self-hostable core is available at github.com/gsavitch/GergVault.
